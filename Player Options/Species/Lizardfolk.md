# Lizardfolk

**Creature Type:** Humanoid

**Size:** Medium (about 5â€“6 feet tall)

**Speed:** 30 feet

As a Lizardfolk, you have these special traits.

**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

**Enhanced Senses.** You have Blindsight with a range of 10 feet.

**Claws.** You can use your claws as natural weapons, they have the finesse property and you count as being proficient with them for Unarmed Strikes. They deal 1d6 slashing damage on a hit.

**Amphibious.** You can breathe water as if it were air and have a Swim Speed equal to your speed.

**Toxicity Tolerance.** Whenever you would take Poison damage, you reduce the damage taken by your proficiency bonus, and you have Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the Poisoned condition.
