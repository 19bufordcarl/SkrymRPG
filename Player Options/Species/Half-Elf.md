# Half-Elf

**Creature Type:** Humanoid

**Speed:** 30 feet

As a Half-Elf, you have these special traits.

**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

**Elven Lineage.** You are part of a lineage that grants you supernatural abilities. Choose a lineage from the Elven Lineages table. You gain the benefit of that lineage. All Elven Lineages also have [Environmental Aptitude](../Origin%20Feats/Environmental%20Aptitude.md) for Poison damage.

Table: Elven Lineages

| Lineage            | Benefit                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Dark Elf           | You have [Environmental Aptitude](../Origin%20Feats/Environmental%20Aptitude.md) for Fire damage.                                           |
| High Elf           | You have [Environmental Aptitude](../Origin%20Feats/Environmental%20Aptitude.md) for Psychic damage.                                        |
| Wood Elf           | You have [Environmental Aptitude](../Origin%20Feats/Environmental%20Aptitude.md) for Necrotic damage.                                       |
| Rare Elven Lineage | You have [Environmental Aptitude](../Origin%20Feats/Environmental%20Aptitude.md) for Acid, Cold, Lightning, Radiant, **or** Thunder damage. |

**Magical Ancestry.** You have Advantage on mental saving throws you make to avoid or end conditions imposed by magical effects.

**Long-lived.** You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.
