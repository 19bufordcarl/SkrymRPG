# Catfolk

**Creature Type:** Humanoid

**Size:** Medium (about 5â€“6 feet tall)

**Speed:** 30 feet

As a Catfolk, you have these special traits.

**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 120 feet.

**Enhanced Senses.** You have Blindsight with a range of 10 feet.

**Claws.** You can use your claws as natural weapons, they have the finesse property and you count as being proficient with them for Unarmed Strikes. They deal 1d6 slashing damage on a hit.

**Feline Agility.** You have proficiency in the Acrobatics skill, have a Climb Speed equal to your speed, and can take no more than 10 damage from falling no matter what height you fall from so long as you are not Incapacitated, Restrained, or Grappled during the fall.
